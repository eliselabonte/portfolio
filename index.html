About Me

I have practiced web design since 2021. But my career did not make its roots in creative beginnings.

I graduated from the Massachusetts Maritime Academy in 2015 with the intention of a long career as a Deck Officer in the Merchant Marine. After flying to Juneau, Alaska the very next day, I spent the following five years doing exactly what I had set out to.

While my time in Alaska was unforgettable, it also brought about the realization that I was a part of an industry that is not sustainable for my own career. I made the difficult decision to leave Alaska and return to New England, to start new.

One cross-country road trip, a summer working with farm animals, and many months of hard work (with more to come) later, I am proud to be able to present a small but growing body of work from my studies.
